DI - Dependency Injection

    IOC - Inversion of Control

    Test Harness - A non-shipping app or web page to exercise a another piece of software.

    Mock - An object or service that looks like the real thing but is smaller, simpler or more local for testing.

    Fixture - Data Generated just for testing.

    Fork vs Branch:
        Fork - A Church like Schism where you take ownership but you may choose to make your stuff available via pull requests.
        Branch - A small place to do a unit of work like a feature or a bug fix that may or may not be merged into the main branch when complete.
